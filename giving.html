Giving Back

Conrad Property Management Inc. is all about providing the best in multi-family property management solutions, but we are also all about giving back to the communities we serve.

We value not only our landlords and tenants but the communities they are connected to, which is why we actively participate in multiple organizations to help make a difference. We strive to be a positive force for our residents and firmly believe in giving back, so we put tremendous value on taking a stand and making meaningful contributions.

Member and President’s Circle Platinum contributor of the Apartment Association of Greater Los Angeles (AAGLA)

AAGLA directly advocates for the rental housing industry by taking action in the city, county, state, and federal government to better serve the rental housing needs of the public. Because we manage multi-family properties, we are especially interested in serving the rental housing needs in a fair manner in order to benefit both our landlords and tenants.

We also actively donate our money and time to Habitat for Humanity because we believe everyone deserves a home to live in.

Habitat for Humanity is a global nonprofit housing organization that works in a number of different ways to create decent, affordable housing for people all over the world. We share the belief that everyone should be able to find an affordable apartment or home, not just in the Los Angeles area, but all around the world. Our employees believe in this core value and volunteer locally to work with Habitat for Humanity to build housing for the less fortunate.

Our CEO, Matthew Conrad, once worked for NBCUniversal and spent many years volunteering for this organization

NBCUniversal’s Discover a Star Foundation supports local organizations and projects that help empower individuals and families to lead more productive and fulfilling lives and has provided financial assistance to over 65 nonprofit organizations. We are proud to be a supporter of such a proactive foundation that provides real, positive change to the communities we work and live in. He knows first-hand how passionate NBCUniversal is towards making a positive impact on the community, especially children and families.
