The Application Process

To apply, please follow this steps

01
Have payment for the application fee of $45.00
02
Provide 2 copies of a valid form of identification
03
Provide three years of residential history, as well as landlord contact information for rental references
04
Provide information on your monthly income*
05
Fill out the full rental online application

*Most property owners require that the applicant’s combined gross monthly income is at least three times the amount of monthly rent.
